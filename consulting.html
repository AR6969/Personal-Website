Core Services

My consulting is centered on two main areas. Both are highly tailored, narrative-driven, and designed around what actually moves outcomes rather than generic templates.

Admissions Strategy

Law School Applications

For law school applicants, I help craft a sharper application strategy built around your story, your strengths, and the specific schools you are targeting. That includes helping you frame your background in a way that feels authentic, compelling, and aligned with the narrative politics and culture of each individual school.

  • Personal statement and essay development, from idea shaping to line-by-line refinement.
  • Narrative positioning so your application tells one coherent story instead of reading like disconnected accomplishments.
  • School-by-school tailoring based on how different programs think about fit, culture, ambition, and professional identity.
  • Resume review, addenda strategy, and help explaining work experience, pivots, gaps, or nontraditional backgrounds the right way.
  • Application strategy focused on where you should lean in, where you should be careful, and how to present yourself with intention.
Recruiting Positioning

Big Law Recruiting

For BigLaw recruiting, I bring a deep interest in V100 / Am Law 100 firms and a strong understanding of how to position candidates based on what kind of practice they want, what office they want, and what story they need to tell. I love talking through firm strategy and helping people map themselves to the right opportunities.

  • Interview preparation and message discipline so you come across polished, thoughtful, and genuinely differentiated.
  • Firm and practice-group positioning across private equity, venture capital, private companies, startups, real estate, public company work, and capital markets.
  • Office strategy based on geography, market dynamics, and how to explain why you want a specific office in a credible way.
  • Resume, networking, and outreach strategy that reflects what firms actually value in candidates with and without prior work experience.
  • Practical advice on how to set yourself apart in a market that increasingly values maturity, commercial instincts, and real professional experience.
